Shorten the Walk


People in Africa, mainly women and
children, spend 40 billion hours every
year walking for water. For $20 a month,
you can give back some of that time by
providing clean water to one person in
Africa - every month!

Promote Health


The first step in addressing HIV/AIDS is
helping people gain knowledge about
their status and letting them know
they're not alone. For $50 a month, you
can provide HIV testing and counseling
to 10 people.

Maximize Impact


Good sanitation and hygiene practices
maximize the impact of clean water. For
$100 a month, you can provide a 4-day
WASH (water, sanitation, and hygiene)
training to equip community members
with the knowledge and skills to improve
their health.

Give a Rain Tank


Rain tanks allow rainwater to be
collected through gutters and safely
stored in cement or plastic tanks. For
$250 a month, you can provide a rain
tank that will store clean water for a
community for months at a time.
